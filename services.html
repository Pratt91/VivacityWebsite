Traveling/Per Diem Coverage

A traveling perfusionist can be a saving grace. Having a highly adaptable and dependable perfusionist to help fill your busy schedule is what we at Vivacity strive for. Contact us if you’re in need and we can send a fully certified perfusionist to help you.

Vivacity offers comprehensive coverage for both adult and pediatric cases. Patient support is critical during open heart surgery which is why provide Perfusion care for all patient populations.

ECMO (Adult/ Pediatric) can be a challenging task for any institution. Vivacity offers 24/7 continuous personalized care and is our company’s standard. As programs develop so will the need to staff them. We have certified Perfusionist here to help train your perfusionist, nursing, and respiratory therapist.
