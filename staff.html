Meet the Team

Dane Pratt CCP

Dane Pratt CCP is a board certified traveling Perfusionist. Prior to getting his degree in Neurobiology From the University of Iowa, he was an Air Crew Chief for the United States Air Force. After graduation, Mr. Pratt found the perfect amalgam of physiology and mechanics in Perfusion. Dane received a Masters in Cardiovascular Perfusion from Rush University.

Tyler Posten CCP

Tyler Posten CCP is a valued member of Vivacity Perfusion. Tyler is a certified practicing perfusionist covering the country. From small hospitals to large universities, he has assisted in covering, training, managing, and streamlining productivity. He has covered numerous accounts from coast to coast, and highly adaptive to many surgical environments.
